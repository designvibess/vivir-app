# Spanish for Life in Spain

A web-first Spanish learning app designed for expats who live, work, or plan to move to Spain. Unlike gamified language apps, this app teaches **Castilian Spanish in real-world contexts** — the language you actually need to rent a piso, talk to your neighbours, order at a bar, or navigate a work meeting in Madrid.

Built with AI-powered adaptive learning, free-form speaking practice, and curriculum grounded in the official **Plan Curricular del Instituto Cervantes** (PCIC).

---

## Why This Exists

Most Spanish learning apps teach Latin American Spanish, gamify progression with hearts and streaks, or focus on travel phrases instead of actual daily life. If you're moving to Spain, those apps leave you saying "carro" instead of "coche," using "ustedes" when you should use "vosotros," and completely unprepared for the rapid-fire directness of Spanish conversation.

We solve this by:
- Focusing on **practical scenarios** you'll face within 48 hours: housing, work, social life, daily errands
- Blending **reading, writing, listening, and speaking** in every lesson
- Adapting to your CEFR level (A1–C2) with continuous assessment
- Providing an AI tutor for unlimited free-form conversation practice at your level

## Key Features

### 📚 Structured Learning Path
- ~66 lessons across A1–B2, covering daily life, work, social situations, and housing
- Every lesson includes a real scenario, dialogue, vocab, grammar, writing, speaking, and cultural context
- Adaptive: difficulty adjusts as you improve

### 🗣️ Conversaciones (Free Speaking Practice)
- Pick any topic, have a real conversation in Spanish with "La Profesora" (AI tutor)
- Get inline corrections when they matter (not nitpicky)
- Session recap shows strengths, improvement areas, and adds new vocab to your SRS deck

### 🎯 Adaptive CEFR Leveling
- Placement test sets your starting level
- System continuously assesses your performance across reading, listening, writing, speaking
- Level automatically adjusts when evidence is strong and sustained
- No gaming: acing below-level content doesn't inflate your level

### 🧠 Spaced Repetition System (SRS)
- Every vocab item and grammar pattern enters your review deck
- SM-2 algorithm schedules reviews at optimal intervals
- Practice items are contextual (sentences from real lessons), not isolated flashcards

### 🇪🇸 Castilian Authenticity
- Vosotros, not ustedes for informal plural
- Spain-specific vocabulary (coche, móvil, ordenador, piso)
- Peninsular idioms (vale, venga, majo, en plan)
- Cultural notes explain the *why* behind Spanish habits

### 🤖 AI-Powered Content
- All lessons, grammar explanations, and placement items generated by a 3-agent pipeline grounded in the PCIC
- Human spot-checked for quality and cultural accuracy
- Pronunciation feedback via speech-to-text + AI scoring
